Virtual Tour Help

Getting Started

Welcome to our virtual museum tour! This immersive experience allows you to explore our galleries as if you were physically present.

Navigation Controls

Move: Click on floor arrows or double-click on the floor
Look Around: Click and drag to change your view
Zoom: Use mouse wheel or pinch gesture
Information: Click on circular info points for details

Special Features

  • Dollhouse view for complete floor plan overview
  • Floor plan navigation for quick room switching
  • High-resolution artifact viewing
  • Audio descriptions and detailed information

Technical Requirements

For the best experience, we recommend using a modern web browser with WebGL support. The tour works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
